NEFFEX

This band is well-known for their consistency in realeasing music. Since the year 2016, they have realeased a song each and every Wednesday: sometimes they published singles, other times it was a part of an album or collection. In 2018, they finished their "100 songs a 100 weeks" challenge, making it their most astonishing achievement, and they haven't stopped releasing songs since.
